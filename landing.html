Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Search across our most common questions, or filter by topic. Still curious? Talk to an agent.

Basics
What is owner-financing, and how is it different from a mortgage?
Owner-financing means Ream — not a bank — finances your purchase directly. You make a deposit, sign a financing contract, and pay a monthly amount until the balance is cleared. It's faster to qualify for than a traditional mortgage (3–5 days vs. 30–90), more flexible on documentation, with no third-party lender. Once you've paid in full, you receive the deed.
Basics
Where in Texas does Ream operate?
Currently, Dallas only. Every property listed on Ream is within Dallas city limits or its immediate surrounds. We plan to expand to Houston and Austin in 2027.
Financing
What's the typical deposit?
Most properties require a 10% deposit, though it can range from 5% to 20% depending on the property and your financial profile. Your indicative offer will show the exact deposit before you commit to anything.
Financing
What rates and terms do you offer?
Fixed APRs from 11.5% to 13.0%, locked at signing. Terms run from 36 months (3 years) up to 240 months (20 years). There are no balloon payments and no early-payment penalties.
Documents & credit
Do you do a credit check?
We do a soft credit pull when you select a property and start the formal application — this does not affect your credit score. We perform a hard pull only at final underwriting, after you've reviewed and accepted your terms.
Documents & credit
What if I'm self-employed or don't have W-2s?
No problem. We accept 12 months of bank statements, your most recent IRS Form 1040, or 1099 records as proof of income. Roughly 40% of our customers are self-employed.
Ownership
What happens if I miss a payment?
Your assigned agent reaches out the day after a missed payment. We offer a 10-day grace period at no charge, and after that a 5% late fee. We never repossess after a single missed payment — we work with you on a forbearance plan if needed.
Ownership
Can I sell or transfer the property before paying it off?
Yes. You can sell, transfer, or refinance with another lender at any time. The remaining balance becomes due at the close of that transaction. We coordinate the title release directly with your buyer's title company.
Legal
How do I know the title is clean?
Every property has its title verified against Dallas County Records before it goes live. The title-verification report is included in your prospect packet, and we use a licensed Texas title company at closing to issue a title insurance policy in your name.
Legal
Is Ream a licensed broker?
Yes. Dispensation Investment Group LLC is licensed by the Texas Real Estate Commission (TREC License #9612045) and our financing operations are registered as a Texas Owner-Finance Mortgage Lender (NMLS #2034581).
