Not sure which guide is yours?

If you signed up after clicking an invite link from REAM staff and your organization is on the platform — you're probably an Organization admin. If you joined an existing team, ask your admin which line you're on (Sales, Finance, or Follow-up). If you applied through a public listing site, you're a Prospect or a Customer.
