About the estate

Goldcrest's flagship land development on the Aburi-Accra ridge. 14 hectares planned across three phases, with paved internal roads, gated security, underground utilities, and a 1.2 ha central park. Phase 1 is fully sold and titled; Phase 2 is mid-launch with 22 of 48 parcels sold; Phase 3 enters approval in Q1 2026.

Master-planned for residential serviced plots, with sunset views over the western valley and direct access to the new N1 link road.
