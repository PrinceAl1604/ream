How daily KPIs are derived

Every completed activity in a template increments a counter on the assigned agent. Mandatory activities count toward the complete-day rate; count-based activities feed numeric KPIs (e.g. calls/day, visits/day); optional activities surface as bonus productivity. KPIs roll up to managers and org dashboards in real time — no extra logging needed.
