What's stored: the invite row binds token to email, role & type. Type is one of SALES, FOLLOW_UP, FINANCE.
Single use: token is consumed at step 6. Re-clicking the link shows an "already used" page. Admin can rotate to issue a new one.
If expired: step 4 shows an expired-link page with a "request a new invite" button that pings the inviter.
Email mismatch: if the invitee tries to sign in with a different email, the request is rejected — invites are bound to the address the admin entered.
What permissions: the role & type from the token determine the dashboard the invitee lands on (Sales queue, Follow-up queue, or Finance ledger).
Audit trail: the inviting admin, the timestamp, the IP that accepted, and the user-agent are all recorded against the invite row.
