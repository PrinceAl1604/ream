Loan documents · Brand · Layout

REAM document template — paper-grade for every loan event.

A single, modular paper layout used by every legally significant document the platform issues — application, estimate, agreement, amortization, payoff and beyond. Brand-consistent letterhead, tokenised type, optional notarisation and stamp blocks, and a reference-number system that ties every document back to its loan ledger.

  1. 1

    Letterhead

    Brand strip with logo, legal entity name, NMLS, address, contact. Same across every document so the issuer is always identifiable.

  2. 2

    Title & reference

    Document title, optional sub-line, and the cross-reference block — Loan ID, Doc ref, Issued. The Doc ref encodes the type (LE, CD, NOTE...).

  3. 3

    Parties

    Borrower & lender (and co-borrower / guarantor when present). Identifiers are masked (SSN ●●●-●●-####) per banking PII rules.

  4. 4

    Body

    Numbered sections (Roman or Arabic) with narrative copy, terms tables, schedules, and .doc-notice callouts for required disclosures.

  5. 5

    Signatures

    Per-party signature lines with name, role, NMLS (where required), and timestamped audit row. Notary block sits underneath when applicable.

  6. 6

    Footer

    Legal entity, mailing address, NMLS, copyright, "Equal Housing Lender", and page numbering. Required by lender regulations.

  7. ★

    Status stamp (optional)

    Rotated stamp pinned to the top-right — SAMPLE, DRAFT, EXECUTED, VOID, PAID IN FULL. Visual shorthand for document state.
